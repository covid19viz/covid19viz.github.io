How Fast Is COVID-19 Spreading in Your Area?

Regional Daily Tracker of the total confirmed cases of COVID-19 per 100k inhabitants, by the number of days since the first confirmed case. The lines are color coded by country (China, Korea, Italy, Spain, Germany, U.S.A., France, UK) and the size of the final dot of the plot line is based on the total number of confirmed cases to date.
